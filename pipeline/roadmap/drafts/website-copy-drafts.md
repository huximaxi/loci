### Page: "Why We Wrote This"
**Subtitle:** A note on the cognitive sovereignty manifesto

We didn't write the manifesto to be contrarian. We wrote it because we kept having the same conversation with the same kinds of people — researchers, developers, privacy advocates, the technically literate — and the conversation always arrived at the same place.

*Where does your thinking live?*

For most people, the honest answer is: somewhere they don't control, maintained by a company whose incentives run perpendicular to theirs, accessed through interfaces designed to maximise engagement rather than understanding.

We think this is the defining infrastructure problem of the current decade. Not AI capability — everyone is working on that. The problem is AI custody. Who holds the model of your mind? Under what terms? With what ability to withdraw consent?

Loci is our answer. Not the only answer. But a specific one: local-first, keypair-signed, protocol-aligned, open. A knowledge garden where the architecture makes extraction difficult rather than merely against the terms of service.

The manifesto is our public declaration of which side we're on. The roadmap is how we prove it.

### Page: "Building in Public — The Sovereignty Roadmap"
**Subtitle:** 12 integrations. One commitment. Q3 starts now.

We are publishing our integration roadmap because we think the sovereignty stack deserves to know what we're building toward — and because we want to be held accountable to it.

The roadmap is not a feature list. It's an alignment declaration. Every integration on it — Nostr, Nym, AT Protocol, Ollama, Goose, Kagi, Tailscale, IPFS — represents a community that has already answered the question we're answering. We're building the bridge between them and the knowledge layer.

**What's shipping in Q3:**
- Ollama local inference — zero API keys, zero cloud, zero telemetry
- MCP server — your garden as context for any agent you trust
- Nostr keypair identity — your key before your first note
- Nym partnership announcement — the metadata layer is coming

**What we're building toward in Q4:**
Eight more integrations. Private sync. Zaps. AT Protocol identity. IPFS export. Tailscale persistent brain.

**What we believe:**
Auth is a feature, not a gate. Sovereignty is never paywalled. When our routing fails, we tell you — we never quietly fall back.

The watcher runs nightly. The tracker is public. When features ship, the roadmap updates.
