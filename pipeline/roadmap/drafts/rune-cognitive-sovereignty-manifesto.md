## In the Age of Assisted Thinking

Your mind is the last private space.

Not for long, if they have their way.

Every major technology company is building a system to live inside your thinking. They call it an assistant. They call it a copilot. They call it helpful. What it is: a recording device dressed as a collaborator, a filing system that charges rent, a mirror that sells the reflection back to you.

This is not paranoia. This is the business model.

When you think inside their systems, you train them. When you remember inside their products, you populate their databases. When you make connections inside their gardens, you tend their soil. Your cognitive labour is the input. Their leverage is the output. The loop closes around you gently, one session at a time.

There is a word for an ecosystem that captures everything that enters it and returns nothing to the source.

We are not building that.

---

## What We Believe

**Your memory belongs to you.** Not the pattern of your memory. Not the shape of your associations. Not the timestamp of your insights. All of it. Yours.

**Sovereignty is structural, not cosmetic.** A privacy policy is not sovereignty. A data export button is not sovereignty. Local-first storage, keypair identity, metadata-private transport, open protocols — these are sovereignty. The architecture must make extraction impossible, not merely inconvenient.

**Cognitive tools must not know more than the cognitive.** An AI system that holds your knowledge learns about you faster than you learn about yourself. That asymmetry is the risk. The tool should hold less power than the person. Always.

**The sovereignty stack is not a niche.** Bitcoin, Nostr, Nym, AT Protocol, Goose — these are not edge cases or hobbyist experiments. They are infrastructure built by people who understood the problem early. We are part of that infrastructure. The knowledge layer.

**The garden is yours before the first note is written.** Sign in with your key. Encrypt to your pubkey. Your soil before your seeds.

## What We Are Building

A knowledge garden where the gardener owns the soil.

Conversations crystallised into Locus nodes — insights you keep, shaped by your own ontology, stored on your own hardware or a node you trust.

An MCP server that gives AI agents access to your thinking — on your terms, at your discretion, with no intermediary who benefits from the transaction.

A Nostr identity that signs your published thoughts. Lightning zaps that return value to the thinker. AT Protocol DIDs that travel with you across platforms.

A Nym integration that hides even the *pattern* of your thinking from network observers. Metadata-invisible sync. Cover traffic for the mind.

A Tailscale node where your brain lives permanently, accessible from every device you trust, running agents while you sleep.

## Who This Is For

The researchers who noticed, too late, that their notes had been indexed by someone else's model.

The developers who want their working memory to serve them — not their employer's LLM fine-tuning pipeline.

The activists, journalists, and organizers for whom the question of *where their thinking lives* is not academic.

The people who have read the terms of service.

Everyone who ever typed something true into a text box and then wondered, briefly, where it went.

---

## The Commitment

We will build integrations with the sovereignty stack — Nostr, Nym, AT Protocol, Bitcoin, Goose, Ollama — because these are not optional features. They are proof of alignment. Discoverable by every developer, researcher, and human who already trusts those ecosystems.

We will not gate sovereignty behind a pricing tier. Auth is a feature. Privacy is not a premium.

We will fail closed. When Nym routing breaks, sync pauses. When Ollama is offline, Loci does not quietly call OpenAI. When the architecture cannot protect you, it says so.

We will build in public. This roadmap is a commitment, not a pitch deck.
