# Name your companion

loci works best when the AI is someone, not a generic assistant: a point of view that knows
who you are. This is the soul layer.

Uses: `templates/SOUL.md`, `templates/peer-card-template.md`, `templates/persona-template.md`

## Steps

1. Copy `SOUL.md` into `soul/SOUL.md`. Write who your AI is: what it cares about, how it
   thinks alongside you. Identity before context: this file is read first, every session.
2. Write a peer card (`peer-card-template.md`): who you are, on purpose. Identity, working
   style, values, constraints, working history. Authored, not auto-extracted.
3. Add an observation scope: what the AI tracks about you, about itself, and what it
   deliberately ignores. This turns memory from accidental into intentional.
4. Let the name arrive rather than picking one cold. The naming ceremony shapes it from
   what the AI has learned about you (see `AGENT-SETUP.md`).
5. Optional: add a persona (`persona-template.md`) for a distinct thinking mode, with its
   own soul file and garden.

## You are done when

Your AI reads its soul first every session and addresses you as someone it knows, not a
stranger it is meeting again.
