# Grow the garden

The garden is where ideas live before they are facts. You plant a question, water it
across sessions, and the strong ones graduate into crystals.

Uses: `templates/garden-template.md`, `templates/garden-file-template.md`,
`templates/garden-health-template.md`

## Steps

1. Create a `garden/` folder in your palace. Copy `garden-file-template.md` for your first
   plant: one file per idea, so nothing collides and nothing merge-conflicts.
2. Name the plant as a question or a hunch, not a conclusion. Give it a stage:
   seed, sapling, plant, crystal-ready.
3. Water it: when a session touches the idea, add a line. The file keeps the whole arc.
4. Run a health pass (trigger the garden-health process). It surfaces stale plants and
   ones ready to graduate. It never archives on its own. It proposes, you decide.
5. When a plant is solid, promote it to a crystal (see `your-first-crystal.md`).

## You are done when

You have at least one plant with a stage, and a health pass has surfaced what to water,
retire, or promote. Ideas cultivated, not just stored.
