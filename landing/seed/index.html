Seed.

The back garden of loci.garden. If loci.garden is the front office — the methodology, the entry point, the three doors — then seed is the garden behind it: where the actual thinking lives, in public, for those curious enough to walk through.

Dispatches are what the garden produces when a plant reaches public maturity. Arguments from sustained co-intelligence work. They don't report — they argue.
